STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)
Document Code: SOP-OPS-12
Title: Emergency Isolation & De-pressurization of High-Pressure Hydrocarbon Pumps
Target Equipment: PUMP-101A

PRE-MAINTENANCE ISOLATION CHECKLIST:
1. Verify Permit to Work (PTW) is signed by the Shift In-Charge.
2. Stop the driver motor. Turn off breaker at MCC Panel Room breaker #MCC-P101. Install lock and tag "DO NOT OPERATE - MAINTENANCE IN PROGRESS".
3. Close the suction and discharge block valves manually.
4. Locate and manually lock out electrical valve VALVE-102 (Discharge Isolation Control Valve). Apply padlocks.
5. Open the bleed valves to drain trapped hydrocarbon fluids to the closed blowdown system (Double Block & Bleed protocol).
6. Verify zero pressure on local gauge PG-101.
7. Conduct gas testing to ensure LEL (Lower Explosive Limit) is 0% before opening the pump housing.

REGULATORY NOTE:
All steps in this checklist comply strictly with the safety protocols of OISD-GDN-115 for hydrocarbon fluid systems.
