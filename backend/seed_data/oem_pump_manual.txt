TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION SHEET: CENTRIFUGAL PROCESS PUMP
Equipment Tag: PUMP-101A
Manufacturer: Flowserve Corp.
Standard: API 610 (Heavy Duty Refinery Service)

OPERATING LIMITS:
- Maximum Operating Pressure: 150 PSI (10.3 bar)
- Maximum Fluid Temperature: 110°C
- Bearing Housing Temperature Threshold: 85°C (185°F) - Critical Alarm Level
- Standard Vibration Warning Limit: 2.8 mm/s velocity (RMS) - Action Required
- Critical Vibration Trip Limit: 4.5 mm/s velocity (RMS) - Automatic Shutdown

TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE (VIBRATION & BEARING TEMPERATURE):
1. Symptom: Bearing temperature > 80°C and rising.
   Possible Cause: Lubricant breakdown or shaft misalignment.
   Action: Verify oil levels. Inspect coupling alignment as detailed in Section 4.
2. Symptom: Shaft vibration velocity > 2.8 mm/s.
   Possible Cause: Rotor imbalance, bent shaft, or coupling misalignment.
   Action: Stop pump, perform dial indicator check on coupling shafts.
