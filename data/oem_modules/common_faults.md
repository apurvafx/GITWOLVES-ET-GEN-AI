# Common Faults

Equipment:
PUMP-101A

---

## Fault

High vibration

Possible Causes

- Misalignment

- Bearing wear

- Pipe strain

Recommended Action

Inspect alignment.

Inspect bearings.

Check piping support.

---

## Fault

High bearing temperature

Possible Causes

- Poor lubrication

- Bearing failure

Corrective Action

Lubricate bearings.

Replace damaged bearing.

---

## Fault

Low discharge flow

Possible Causes

- Blocked suction

- Air entrainment

- Impeller damage

Corrective Action

Inspect suction line.

Inspect impeller.

Remove blockage.
