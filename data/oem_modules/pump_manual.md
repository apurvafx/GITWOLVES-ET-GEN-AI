# OEM Manual – PUMP-101A

## Equipment Information

Equipment Tag: PUMP-101A

Equipment Type:
Frame-mounted centrifugal chemical process pump with magnetic drive.

OEM Reference:
Based on Flowserve CPXS Installation, Operation & Maintenance Manual.

---

## Operating Principle

PUMP-101A is a centrifugal process pump designed for chemical service.

The pump uses a magnetic drive arrangement to prevent product leakage and improve operational safety.

---

## Main Components

- Pump casing
- Open impeller
- Shaft
- Bearing housing
- Magnetic drive
- Bearings
- Electric motor

---

## Operating Limits

- Maximum pump speed:
Refer to equipment nameplate.

- Ambient temperature:
Up to 40°C unless otherwise specified.

- Pump shall not be operated outside rated conditions.

---

## Important Safety Rules

- Never operate beyond design limits.

- Always follow plant safety procedures.

- Read operating instructions before startup.

- Maintenance must be carried out only by trained personnel.

---

## Maintenance Notes

Routine inspection should include:

- Bearing condition

- Lubrication

- Alignment

- Piping inspection

- Pump vibration

- Leakage inspection
