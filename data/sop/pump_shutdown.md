# Shutdown Procedure

Equipment:
PUMP-101A

## Normal Shutdown

1. Slowly close discharge valve.

2. Stop motor.

3. Close suction valve if maintenance is planned.

4. Isolate electrical supply.

5. Lockout / Tagout.

6. Depressurize system.

7. Drain pump if hazardous liquid is present.

8. Record shutdown in maintenance log.
